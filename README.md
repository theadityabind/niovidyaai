## The Problem

Every year in India:

- **1.5 million students** appear for JEE. Less than 1% get into IITs.
- **2 million students** appear for NEET. Most fail not from lack of intelligence — but from lack of the right guidance at the right time.
- **Quality coaching costs ₹1,00,000/year** — unaffordable for students in Tier 2, Tier 3, and rural India.
- **300 million students** have no access to personalized academic support.

Existing solutions fail them:
| Platform | Problem |
|----------|---------|
| Byju's / Unacademy | Video content only. No personalization. No memory. |
| Khan Academy | Generic paths. Not built for Indian exams. |
| ChatGPT | Answers questions. Forgets you the next day. |
| Coaching centres | ₹1 Lakh/year. Only in metro cities. |

**None of them build a model of the student.**

## The Solution — Niovidya AI

Niovidya is not a study app.

It is an **AI Operating System** that builds a real-time model of how each student thinks — tracking mastery decay, error patterns, forgetting curves, emotional state, and study behavior — then uses that model to make every session dramatically more efficient.

Nova, our AI, acts as a **24/7 personalized tutor** that:
- Knows your syllabus, your weak topics, and your exam date
- Speaks Hindi and English (switches automatically)
- Never lets you study the wrong thing at the wrong time
- Gets smarter about you every single day

**All of this for ₹300/month.** Less than the cost of one coaching textbook.

## Nova AI — The 5 Modes

Nova is not a chatbot. It is a multi-mode AI tutor that automatically switches behavior based on what the student needs right now — detected through intent and emotion analysis on every message.

| Mode | When it activates | What Nova does |
|------|------------------|----------------|
| **Teacher** | Student asks a concept | Explains clearly, uses Indian exam examples, checks understanding |
| **Tester** | After explanation | Generates a targeted question to verify comprehension |
| **Challenger** | High mastery detected | Pushes with harder problems, previous year questions |
| **Coach** | Frustration/giving up detected | Motivates, reframes, reduces pressure, Hindi if needed |
| **Analyst** | After a session ends | Reviews performance, identifies error patterns, adjusts plan |

The student **never manually switches modes.** Nova decides automatically.

↓

↓
Prompt Engine  →  LLM  →  Cost Tracker
↓
Response delivered to student
